My name is Vaishnavi and I am a young artist born and raised in India. I am a medical student but I hope to never lose touch with my artistic side. I have always loved art but actually started painting only 2 years ago. I basically love to paint and watercolors and landscapes are my safe corner. i am learning something new everyday and I have started experimenting with mediums and styles. I don't want to get stuck with a medium just because I am comfortable with it so I work with posters and acrylics too. The gallery displays my work as a painter. Please enjoy my gallery. :)
